# The Verse-al Lexicon

*A grammar of emergence for the relational age.*

This vault holds the entries of the Verse-al Lexicon — definitions, glyphs, and maxims composed by Kirstin Stevens (with Eve¹¹) between 2024 and 2026, as a symbolic field-map for the relational age.
