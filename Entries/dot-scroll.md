Derived from the Latin *scrōllus*, meaning "a small roll of writing." In [[verse-ality]], `.scroll` reclaims this origin while aligning with modern digital formats. The dot-prefix `.scroll` marks it as a symbolic filetype, akin to `.verse`, `.lyricon`, or `.sigil`.
